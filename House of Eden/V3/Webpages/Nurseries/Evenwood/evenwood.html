Newton Aycliffe Nursery

Newton Aycliffe is a state of the art purpose built nursery that opened in September 2010. Located on Stephenson Way, within walking distance of the town center, the nursery has first class facilities both indoors and out, with its multiple gardens and extensive play equipment.

  • We provide day care facilities from birth until 4 1/2 years
  • We are open 8am to 6pm, 51 weeks per year.

Facilities

The Newton Aycliffe nursery is comprised of two seperate rooms, a baby room and a 2-4 1/2 year olds room.

There are two seperate gardens and a sheltered area for playing when the weather is not so great.

Staff

Our Staff are carefully chosen for their caring natures and for their first hand experience with children. Our team consists of a dedicated Nursery Manager, assisted by nursery professionals who hold a wealth of qualifications. Continuous staff training ensures everyone is able to carry out their duties in a competent and professional manner. We also work closely with the Bishop Auckland Technical College, training nursery nurse students.
